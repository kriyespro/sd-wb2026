FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Ask Us Anything

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. Brand identity: 3–4 weeks, website: 6–10 weeks, mobile app: 12–20 weeks. We provide a full timeline during the discovery call.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes! All plans include 30-day post-launch support. Enterprise clients get 90 days. We also offer ongoing retainer packages for continuous growth and maintenance.

What industries do you specialize in?

We work across technology, healthcare, e-commerce, finance, education and lifestyle. Our diverse portfolio reflects our ability to adapt to any domain or vertical.

Can I see work samples before we begin?

Absolutely. Our portfolio spans multiple industries. We can share relevant case studies specific to your sector during our initial consultation call.

What is your payment structure?

We work milestone-based: 40% upfront, 40% at mid-project review, 20% on final delivery. Custom arrangements are available for enterprise clients.
